## 3. Competitive Strategy: How to Beat RERAScore

### Strategy 1: Win on Geography First

RERAScore has ZERO coverage of Haryana and Delhi. Haryana-RERA covers Gurgaon — where property prices have risen 150%+ since 2019 and luxury home sales dominate NCR. Delhi-RERA covers the capital.

**Action:** Build Haryana-RERA and Delhi-RERA scrapers FIRST. Then add UP-RERA (which RERAScore already covers, but we'll do it better with our commercial features). This gives us NCR dominance from Day 1.

### Strategy 2: Win on Monetization (They Have None)

RERAScore appears to be a passion project or early-stage venture with no visible revenue model. Every feature we build should have a monetization layer baked in:

- Every project page has a lead capture form ("Interested? Get a callback")
- Every builder profile has a "Claim This Profile" CTA leading to paid plans
- Every page has optimized ad placements (header, sidebar, in-content)
- Search results include sponsored listings at the top
- Builder comparison tool upsells premium analytics
- Locality pages drive affiliate revenue (home loans, legal services)

### Strategy 3: Win on Content & SEO Depth

RERAScore has minimal content beyond project data pages. Thousands of high-value keywords are up for grabs:

| Content Type | Example Keywords | Monthly Search Volume | RERAScore Coverage |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| How-to Guides | how to check RERA status, how to file RERA complaint | 10,000–50,000 | NONE |
| Builder Reviews | [builder name] reviews, is [builder] reliable | 500–5,000 per builder | NONE (no user reviews) |
| Locality Reports | best projects in Gurgaon, new launches in Noida 2026 | 2,000–20,000 per locality | NONE |
| Comparison Articles | [project A] vs [project B] | 100–1,000 per comparison | Basic tool, no articles |
| News/Updates | RERA news today, new RERA rules 2026 | 5,000–15,000 | NONE |
| Investment Guides | is [city] good for real estate investment | 5,000–25,000 | NONE |
| NRI Guides | NRI property investment India | 2,000–10,000 | NONE |

**Total addressable keyword opportunity: 50,000–100,000+ keywords that RERAScore is not targeting.**

### Strategy 4: Win on Trust Score 2.0 (Better Methodology)

Our scoring is superior with 8 dimensions (vs their 6):

| Dimension | Weight | Source | RERAScore Has This? |
|-----------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Delivery Track Record | 25 pts | RERA data | Yes (30pts) |

**Brand Position:** While RERAScore gives you a trust score, we give you a "Complete Property Intelligence Report" — covering RERA compliance + market data + neighbourhood quality + builder financials.

### Strategy 5: Win on User Experience & Speed

- Instant search with typo tolerance (Meilisearch) — results as you type
- Interactive maps: view projects on a map with score-based color coding
- Project timeline visualization: interactive chart showing every status change
- Builder comparison dashboard: drag-and-drop builders to compare visually
- Mobile-first design with PWA (installable, offline-capable)
- Dark mode
- Hindi language support from Day 1 (RERAScore is English-only)

## The Bottom Line

RERAScore has validated the market and built a solid data foundation. But they've left a massive commercial opportunity on the table. Our platform doesn't need to out-data them — it needs to out-monetize, out-content, and out-serve them. By launching in markets they don't cover (Haryana, Delhi), with features they don't have (dashboards, leads, ads, Hindi, WhatsApp), and a revenue model they haven't built (subscriptions, leads, self-serve ads) — we can capture the commercial value of the RERA transparency market that RERAScore is currently giving away for free.
